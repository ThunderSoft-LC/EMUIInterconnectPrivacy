Statement on EMUI Interconnect and Privacy

Updated: November 23, 2025

"EMUI Interconnect" is a cross-platform large-file transfer application provided by Thunder Software Technology Co., Ltd. (registered address: No. 9 Qinghua East Road, Haidian District, Beijing (Chuangda Building)) (hereinafter referred to as "we" or "ThunderSoft"). ThunderSoft attaches great importance to your personal information and privacy. We will provide appropriate security protection for your personal information in accordance with legal requirements and industry‑standard security practices.

Summary

You can visit the "List of Personal Information Collected" to quickly understand the purpose, method, and scope of the collection and use of personal information by "EMUI Interconnect".

• To provide a better EMUI Interconnect service, we need to collect: device identifier, device model, device name, system version, and network information (local IP address, gateway IP address).

• To protect the rights of ThunderSoft, you, or other ThunderSoft users, we will use the above collected information for risk control, prevention of fraud and other illegal activities, and to reduce credit risk.

Your transferred data contains your sensitive personal information (photo albums, contact lists, etc.). We will only access them after you have given explicit consent in the application. You may uninstall this application to terminate the collection or processing of the above data or functions. Your above data will be stored exclusively within the territory of the People's Republic of China.

1. How We Collect and Use Your Personal Information

(a) Your device information: When you use this service on a particular device, we collect specific information from that device, including device identifier, device model, device name, system version, and network information (local IP address, gateway IP address) to identify the version, quickly diagnose errors, troubleshoot issues, ensure functional compatibility, improve the product in a timely manner, and provide version update notifications.

(b) Your log information: During your use of this service, we will collect service logs (including usage duration, amount of transferred data, data type, operation status, connection status, and other behavioral log information). The log information collected from you will be used solely to provide the basic service, analyze problems and errors that may arise during service use, and will not be linked to your personal identity.

(c) Location information: When devices connect, your location information is needed to establish the connection and discover devices, in order to complete the service function you require. Location information is only cached locally, not uploaded to servers, and not used for any unauthorized purposes.

(d) User transferred data: When you use the file transfer and other related functions, we need to cache the images, videos, texts, contacts you have selected, as well as the permission status of the application or service module you are transferring, to help you complete the transfer of such data content. The relevant data is used solely to complete the transfer; we do not perform content recognition, intent analysis, or use it for any other purpose.

We will provide you with the following business functions through EMUI Interconnect. During the use of the relevant functions, we will process the following information necessary to provide the function, in order to fulfill our contractual obligations. If you do not provide the relevant information, it may affect your use of the related functions of this application.

1.1 Mutual File Transfer

(1) When you use the mutual file transfer function, we need to collect your device identifier (UUID), device model, and Bluetooth address to distinguish you from your other device and to establish pairing.

(2) Through this function, you can establish a connection with another device to receive contacts, files, images, videos, and other supported content from that device. To achieve this purpose, we need to read the user content you have selected. Such content will be automatically collected and processed by us after your consent when you use the mutual file transfer function.

1.2 Help and Feedback

The "Help and Feedback" function provides you with solutions to common issues. When you use the "Help and Feedback" function, we collect your device hardware information (device model, device type) and the description of the problem to provide you with appropriate results and answer your questions.

2. Device Permission Requests

Location permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to determine whether you are on the same local network. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

Local network and wireless data permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to connect devices and transfer files on the same local network.

Bluetooth permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to search for nearby devices via Bluetooth.

Real-time activity permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to show transfer progress via Dynamic Island when the application is in the background. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

Contacts permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to read or write contact information. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

Photos permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to read or write image and video information. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

Camera permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable this permission, which is used to take photos with the camera to send feedback. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

Document storage permission: When you use the mutual transfer function, you may choose to enable permissions such as photos, camera, and contacts, to read or write images, videos, and file information used by the application. If you do not need such service, you may turn off this permission at any time.

After obtaining your consent, we will provide you with the mutual transfer service and process the relevant information.

3. Protection of Minors

We attach great importance to the protection of minors' personal information. ThunderSoft will strictly comply with national laws and regulations when providing services to minors and protecting them. If you are a minor, you need the consent of your parents or other guardians to use this application and accept the relevant terms of service. Parents or other guardians should also take appropriate preventive measures to protect the minor, including supervising their use of this application.

In particular, if you are a child (a minor under the age of fourteen), before using our services, please obtain the consent or guidance of your parents or other guardians before using our services or providing information to us. If you are a parent or guardian of a child, please ensure that the child under your care uses our services and provides information to us with your consent or guidance.

4. Sharing with Third Parties

To provide you with certain functions/services, for example, to display file information, contact information in Huawei Share, we need to provide your personal information to third parties. To ensure the security of your data and privacy, we have signed data security protection agreements with our business partners, which specify the partners' responsibilities, obligations, and requirements for protecting your data. For details, please refer to the "Personal Information Sharing List".

4.1 Withdrawal of Consent

If you wish to revoke permission, you may go to "Settings" > "Permission Management" and turn off the corresponding switch to withdraw your consent.

5. Location of Information Storage

5.1 Storage Location

(1) Your device information, location information, and user transferred data will be stored locally on the device.

(2) Your log information and feedback content will be stored within the territory of the People's Republic of China.

5.2 Storage Period

We retain your personal information only for as long as necessary to achieve the purposes stated in this Statement. After you cancel the service, delete the data yourself, or the retention period expires, we will promptly delete or anonymize your personal information, unless otherwise required by laws and regulations (for example, the Cybersecurity Law requires network logs to be retained for 6 months). In addition, when our products or services cease operation, we will notify you via push notifications, announcements, etc.
