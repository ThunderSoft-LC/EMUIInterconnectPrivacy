1. Transfer of User Content

(1) You acknowledge that this Service is used to transfer contacts, images, music, videos, and other content supported by this Service (collectively "User Content") from one device to another authorised device.

(2) When you transfer a type of User Content for the first time (for example, when you transfer photos from your device for the first time), we will ask for your prior authorisation to access that content.

(3) Some User Content will be displayed on the interface of this Service in the form of images, summaries, or other tangible forms. The specific display will be randomly generated and updated based on the User Content stored on your device.

(4) User Content may only be transferred to the specific device authorised to receive the content in a single transfer. With respect to any User Content, we only provide a service that helps transfer from one device to another; we do not collect it from you, nor do we obtain, process, or use it in any way.

2. Information We Collect

(a) Your device information: When you use this Service on a particular device, we collect specific information from that device, including device identifier, device model, device name, system version, and network information (local IP address, gateway IP address) to identify the version, quickly diagnose errors, troubleshoot issues, ensure functional compatibility, improve the product in a timely manner, and provide version update notifications.

(b) Your log information: During your use of this Service, we will collect service logs (including usage duration, amount of transferred data, data type, operation status, connection status, and other behavioral log information). The log information collected from you will be used solely to provide the basic service, analyze problems and errors that may arise during service use, and will not be linked to your personal identity.

(c) Location information: When devices connect, your location information is needed to establish the connection and discover devices, in order to complete the service function you require. Location information is only cached locally, not uploaded to servers, and not used for any unauthorized purposes.

(d) User transferred data: When you use the file transfer and other related functions, we need to cache the images, videos, texts, contacts you have selected, as well as the permission status of the application or service module you are transferring, to help you complete the transfer of such data content. The relevant data is used solely to complete the transfer; we do not perform content recognition, intent analysis, or use it for any other purpose.

3. Mutual File Transfer

(1) When you use the mutual file transfer function, we need to collect your device identifier (UUID), device model, and Bluetooth address to distinguish you from your other device and to establish pairing.

(2) Through this function, you can establish a connection with another device to receive contacts, files, images, videos, and other supported content from that device. To achieve this purpose, we need to read the user content you have selected. Such content will be automatically collected and processed by us after your consent when you use the mutual file transfer function.
