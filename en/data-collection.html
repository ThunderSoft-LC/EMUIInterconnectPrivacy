List of Collected Personal Information

Thundersoft (hereinafter referred to as "we") strictly complies with national laws and regulations, integrating legal and compliant practices into product design and business processes, and employs advanced industry security technologies to ensure data security. At the same time, we highly value your personal information and privacy protection, and are committed to defending everyone's privacy rights. To this end, we adhere to five basic principles of privacy protection:

●  Data Minimization: Insist on collecting only the personal data necessary to realize business functions to serve your needs.

●  On-Device Data Processing: Strive to complete the processing and analysis of personal data on the user's device whenever possible.

●  Transparency and Control: When collecting personal data, clearly and explicitly inform users, and ensure they know how the data is used and how to opt out.

●  Identity Protection: Use privacy-enhancing technologies to hide the user's identity when data leaves the user's device.
