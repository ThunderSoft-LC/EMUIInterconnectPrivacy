1. Transmission of User Content

(1) You confirm that this Service is used to transmit contacts, pictures, music, videos, and other content supported for transfer on the device (collectively referred to as "User Content") to another licensed device.

(2) When you first transfer a certain type of User Content (for example, when you first transfer photos from your device), we will obtain your prior authorization for accessing such content.

(3) Some User Content may be displayed on the interface of this Service in the form of images, summaries, or other tangible forms. Specific display situations will be randomly generated and updated based on the User Content stored on your device.

(4) User Content can only be transmitted to a specific device authorized to receive User Content in a single transfer. For any User Content, we only provide services to help transfer from one device to another. We do not collect it from you, nor do we acquire, process, or use it in any form.

2. Information We Collect

(a) Your Device Information: When you use this Service on a device, we collect specific information from that device, including Device Identifier, Device Model, Device Name, System Version, and Network Information (Local IP Address, Gateway IP Address). This is used to identify the version, quickly diagnose errors, troubleshoot issues, and provide you with compatibility judgments to optimize the product promptly and provide version update notifications.

(b) Your Log Information: During your use of this Service, we will collect log information of this service (including usage duration, data transfer volume, data type, operation status, connection status, and other behavioral log information). The log information collected from you will only be used to provide basic services, analyze problems and errors that have occurred or may occur during service use, and will not be associated with your personal identity.

(c) Location Information: When devices connect, they need to connect and retrieve devices based on your location information to complete the functional services you require. Location information is only cached locally and will not be uploaded to servers or used for other unauthorized purposes.

(d) User Transfer Data: When you use functions like file transfer, we need to cache the images, videos, text, contacts, and other content you select, as well as your authorization status for the application or service modules you are transferring, to help you complete the transfer of the aforementioned data content. Relevant data is only used to complete the transfer; we do not perform content recognition, intent recognition, nor use it for any other purpose.

3. File Transfer

(1) When you use the file transfer function, we need to collect your Device ID (UUID), Device Model, and Bluetooth Address to distinguish and pair with your other device.

(2) You can use this function to establish a connection with another device to receive contacts, files, images, videos, and other supported content from that device. To achieve the aforementioned purpose, we need to read the aforementioned user content selected by you. This content will be automatically collected and processed by us with your consent when you use the file transfer function.

4. Permission Requirements

The implementation of this Service's functions requires enabling certain device permissions. The specific permissions and authorization purposes are as follows:

(2) Location Permission: Used to determine if devices are on the same local area network.

(3) Local Network, Wireless Data Permission: Used to connect devices and transfer files under the same local area network.

(4) Bluetooth Permission: Used to use Bluetooth to search for nearby devices.

(1) Real-time Activity Permission: Used to display transfer progress via the Dynamic Island when in the background.
